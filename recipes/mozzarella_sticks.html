Fried Mozzarella Sticks

Description

The perfect table snack to serve at parties. Quick and easy to make, these deep-fried mozzarella sticks are the perfect blend of crispy on the outside and gooey on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 2 cups oil for frying, or as needed
  • 1(16 ounce) package mozzarella cheese sticks

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk water and eggs together in a small bowl. Mix bread crumbs and garlic salt together in medium bowl. Blend flour and cornstarch together in a third bowl.
  3. Heat oil to 365 degrees F(185 degrees C) in a large, heavy saucepan.
  4. Dredge a mozzarella stick in flour; shake off excess. Dip into egg mixture. Lift it up so excess egg drips back into the bowl. Press into bread crumbs to coat. Place breaded mozzarella sticks on a plate or wire rack. Repeat with remaining mozzarella sticks.
  5. Use a spider spoon or a pair of tongs to lower 3 to 4 mozzarella sticks into the hot oil. Fry until golden brown, about 30 seconds. Remove from heat and drain on paper towels. Repeat to fry remaining mozzarella sticks.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
